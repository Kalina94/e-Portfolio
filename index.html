About Me

I am an IT engineer for a bank in Mauritius. Dealing with confidential data in my work, I am mindful of the data protection. This is the primary reason why I am interested in studying the MSc Cyber Security. I also feel that, by studying this course, I would acquire new skills as well as an understanding and familiarity with Cyber-Security to enable me to fulfil the ambition of working within the security industry. Regarding my interest of Computer Security, it stems from previous study of Computer Forensics as a degree module in which I explored the history of computer forensics, computer crime activities and computer crime methods which was my most successful university assignment. Following this assignment, I chose the same area for my final year project, which was based on building forensic workstations for android phones.
